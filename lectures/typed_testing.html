Testing Akka Typed Behaviors

We have seen in previous weeks that testing actors in their natural habitat involves asynchronous tests, timeouts, and non-determinism. Since Akka Typed uses pure behavior functions, we can write deterministic tests without depending on timing, as demonstrated in this lecture.
